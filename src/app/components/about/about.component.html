Event & Meetings

Who we are?

Originally it was a Gibsons United Church until December 2016. With sweat and love, the owners were able to successfully renovate the property and convert it into an event space with an aim to bring it up to the use of community. Its unique character and appealing design makes it and perfect location for weddings, celebration of life, art, music events, meetings etc. This property is close to Gibsons Marina, beach and Gibsons Public Market.
